Why Choose Us?

Jivan and Co. was started by Umesh Jivan on the 1st October 1989 after he had worked as a professional assistant in two city law firms. The firm's first place of business was at Sangro House in Smith Street, Durban. Veena Sheik joined the firm on the 1st January 1990 as an Office Administrator.
