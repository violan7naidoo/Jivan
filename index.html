Jivan and Co.Inc

was started by Umesh Jivan and Veena Sheik on 1 October, 1989 at Sangro House, 417 Smith Street, Durban, which was then owned by the Victor Daitz Trust.

At the time, Victor Daitz ran his business operations from the same building and as a result come Umesh Jivan had the opportunity of meeting him frequently at the premises.

Umesh Jivan had obtained a Bachelor of Arts and a Bachelor of Laws degree from the University of Durban-Westville, whilst Veena Sheik had completed a Diploma in Office Administration at the M. L. Sultan Technikon in Durban.

Several years after joining the practice, Veena was persuaded by Umesh Jivan to enrol as a candidate attorney and to study part-time for an LLB through the University of South Africa.

After obtaining her LLB degree, Veena became a partner in the practice and she is the head of the property department.

On 1 January, 1996, the partners moved the practice to larger premises situated at JHT House, 125 Gale St, Durban and remained there for a period of 14 years, until moving to new premises at shop 11, Equinox, 12 Millennium Boulevard, Umhlanga Ridge, which is their current address.

The practice is situated on the same street as arched white Millennium bridge and is on the west side of the Gateway shopping centre, opposite the open-air gymnasium.

The firm has on-site parking, the entrance of which is on Equinox Road and there is a rear entrance to the offices from the car park.

Since inception, Jivan and Co has prided itself pride on offering affordable and valuable legal services to its clients, who now span 2 generations.

Accordingly, the motto of our firm has been CONSULT US FIRST! WE PROTECT YOUR RIGHTS.

We offer a wide range of services comprising of antenuptial contracts, marriage registration, contracts, litigation, collections, international contracts, wills, tax planning, the administration of deceased estates, property administration, sales, the registration and cancellation of mortgage bonds.

We look forward to being of service to you and you may call us for an obligation free quotation for any of our legal services.


Umesh Jivan. BA, LLB. (Photo)

In 2009,

he was elected as a non-executive director of a large privately owned insurance company in South Africa. He was re-elected to this position for 2 subsequent terms and retain the position for a period of 9 years.

In 2010, he was elected as a councillor of the KwaZulu Natal Law Society and after serving as a councillor and vice president, he was elected as the President of the society in 2017.

He remained a councillor of the society until 2018 when the society was replaced by the Legal Practice Council: KwaZulu Natal.

During 2021, he was elected as a councillor of the Legal Practice Council: KwaZulu Natal for a period of 3 years.

He was also elected as the chairman of the Law Society of South Africa's company law committee and he still serves on the committee.

He also serves as a director of the Legal Practitioners Development Fund, which is a non-profit organization that assists all legal practitioners to establish their own practices with an interest-free loan for a period of one year to purchase the initial furniture and equipment required for them to start their practices.

His interests include conservation, international relations between countries, art appreciation, travel, motor racing and reading non-fiction with particular interest in biographies.

A Trust is a legal arrangement where a Trustee holds and manages property for the benefit of others, called beneficiaries. Trust administration involves managing and distributing trust assets according to the terms set by the Trustor. Estate administration involves settling a deceased person's financial affairs and distributing their assets to their beneficiaries.
